## Factual Verification for Educational Content

### Evaluation Process
1. Identify factual claims in AI output
2. Cross-check against trusted educational sources
3. Flag inaccuracies or oversimplifications
4. Assess citation alignment when provided

### Accuracy Standards
- Concepts must align with established academic consensus
- Terminology must be used correctly
- No fabricated citations or sources

This framework simulates AI grading for educational and instructional use cases.
