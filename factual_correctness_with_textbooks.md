# Factual Correctness Evaluation Using Textbooks

This document outlines the methodology for verifying the accuracy of AI-generated content
against trusted educational sources.

---

## 1. Overview
- Ensures AI responses align with **authoritative textbooks** or public reference materials.
- Evaluates correctness, completeness, and proper use of terminology.
- Demonstrates transferable skills in **AI content validation** and educational QA.

---

## 2. Evaluation Steps
1. Identify factual statements in AI output.
2. Compare against textbook references or reliable public sources.
3. Categorize each statement as:
   - ✅ Correct
   - ⚠ Minor Error
   - ❌ Major Error
4. Flag discrepancies for review and note improvements.

---

## 3. Example
**Prompt:** "Explain the phases of mitosis."  
**AI Response:** "Prophase, metaphase, interphase, telophase."  
**Evaluation:**  
- Interphase is incorrectly included as a mitosis phase → Major Error  
- Other phases correctly listed.

**Action:** Flag for correction and provide accurate reference.

---

## 4. Notes
- Focuses on factual correctness, not style or clarity.
- Applies to text generated for **educational content, tutorials, or reference material**.
- Uses simulated prompts and public resources only.
